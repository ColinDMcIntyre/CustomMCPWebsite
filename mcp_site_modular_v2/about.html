About

Built by a real person for real use

I built this for myself because I wanted a simple way to connect AI to real tools without paying for another monthly middleman. After I got it working for my own use, I cleaned it up, made it modular, and documented it so other people could use it too.

Why I made it

I wanted direct control over my own setup. I did not want to rely on another service in the middle, and I wanted something I could keep building on myself.

That is why this is built to run on Render, why the instructions are written out clearly, and why the project is easy to expand with more Python tools later.

What you can expect

If you buy one of these plans, you are dealing directly with me. No bot, no sales team, and no outsourced support.

If you have setup questions, want help choosing a plan, or need something more custom, you can contact me directly by email or phone.
